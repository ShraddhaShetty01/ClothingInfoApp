ABOUT US

With Student dormitory Heidelberg you will find the dorms you are looking for throughout Heidelberg. Because we gather the information from the students about the preference of rooms (Single or Double occupancy) and show the results based on it.Students will not have to go through the tedious search on multiple platforms. Here you will find offers from the classic rental apartment in the city.
